1. Problem

Top 3 Problems:

  • Insufficient access to capital for urban transitions.
  • Lack of scalable digital infrastructure for secure data & app sharing.
  • Fragmented markets for financial partners and solution providers.

Key Assumptions:

  • €1.5B total finance demand for 55 municipalities.
  • High transaction costs and inefficiencies in current bond markets.
  • Average project size: ~€6.7M.
2. Solution

A unified ecosystem with three revolutionary hubs, built on one trusted foundation.

  • Urban Transitions Hub: Aggregates municipal funding needs.
  • Bond Issuance Hub: AI-automates bond creation.
  • Digital Accelerator Hub: "App Store for Cities".
6. Key Metrics

Success Metrics:

  • Municipalities onboarded (6 → 50 → 600).
  • Financial partners actively developing deals.
  • Capital mobilized (€1.5B across 225 projects).
  • ESG impact delivered (GCoM/CDP aligned).

Financial Metrics:

  • Platform usage and engagement.
  • Revenue from enablement services.
  • Cost reduction for participants.
3. Unique Value Proposition

The first integrated platform enabling municipalities to access blended finance and share data/apps in a digital ecosystem that drives urban transition innovation, while empowering financial partners to develop the high-demand bond market through secure, Gaia-X compliant infrastructure.

4. Unfair Advantage

Hard to Copy:

  • Integrated partner ecosystem (DB, T-Sys, AFS).
  • Gaia-X compliance early mover advantage.
  • Dual-sided value creation.

9. Cost Structure

CAPEX (One-Time Investments):

  • Primary Platform Development by T-Systems.
  • Strategic Partner Integration setup (e.g., Deutsche Börse, AFS).

OPEX (Recurring Costs):

  • Platform Operations & Hosting (T-Systems).
  • OPCO Management (GTM & partner teams).
  • Recurring Partner Service Fees (e.g., market data APIs).
8. Revenue Streams

Platform Memberships (Tiered Model):

  • Financial Partners (Standard & Premium Tiers).
  • Municipalities (Single flat annual fee).
  • Solution Providers (Single flat annual fee).

Enablement Services (Transaction-Based):

  • Deal Accelerator Services (e.g., €40k avg. fee per project).
  • Municipal Success Fees (e.g., 0,40% avg. of issuance value).
